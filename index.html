About Us


Welcome to UNITED HAIR STUDIO, a contemporary men's salon situated in the heart of Newcastle upon Tyne, just a stone's throw away from St James Park. Our studio has been thoughtfully designed to provide a serene and rejuvenating atmosphere, allowing you to unwind and relax while our skilled barbers take care of your hair.


At UNITED HAIR STUDIO, we pride ourselves on our ability to cater to any style, whether you're after a classic cut or the latest trend in men's hair. Our barbers are highly trained and experienced, able to work with all hair types and textures to create a look that is uniquely you.


We understand that your hair is an important aspect of your image and personal style, which is why we take the time to listen to your needs and provide tailored advice to achieve your desired outcome. Whether you're after a skinfade or a taper fade we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.


Book your appointment with UNITED HAIR STUDIO today and experience the ultimate in modern male grooming. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out to us. Our team is always happy to help, and you can contact us through our Instagram page or by giving us a call. We guarantee you'll leave feeling and looking your best, ready to take on the world with confidence.
